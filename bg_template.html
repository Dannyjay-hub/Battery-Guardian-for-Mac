About Battery Guardian

Battery Guardian is a forensic tool that detects counterfeit, reprogrammed, or spoofed MacBook batteries by analyzing the Texas Instruments gas gauge chip data.

Health Score: A composite 0-100 rating combining capacity, cycle count, and spoof indicators.

Entropy Check: Verifies that individual cells have natural capacity variance (spoofed batteries show zero).

Live Sensor Test: Monitors voltage fluctuation in real-time. Real batteries fluctuate; fakes show flatlines.

Odometer Check: Compares cycle count against flash write count to detect resets.

Time Paradox: Detects if the internal clock has been frozen to hide the battery's true age.
